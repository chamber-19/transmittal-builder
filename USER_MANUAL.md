# Transmittal Builder User Manual

This guide is for end users who install and run the desktop `.exe`.

---

## 1. Install The App

1. Open the shared drive folder:

   ```text
   G:\Shared drives\R3P RESOURCES\APPS\Transmittal Builder\
   ```

2. Run the latest installer:

   ```text
   Transmittal.Builder_<version>_x64-setup.exe
   ```

3. If Windows SmartScreen appears, click **More info** then **Run anyway**.

4. Complete the installer prompts.

---

## 2. First Launch

On launch, the app checks for updates and opens normally when up-to-date.

If your organization has PIN activation enabled for the installed build, you
will see a PIN activation screen before entering the app.

---

## 3. PIN Activation (If Prompted)

1. Enter the PIN provided by your administrator.
2. Click activate.
3. On success, the app opens.

Notes:

- PINs are machine-bound and cannot be reused on a different machine.
- Activation tokens are time-based; you may be prompted to re-activate when a
  token expires.

If your PIN fails:

- Verify you typed it exactly.
- Contact your administrator for a new/active PIN.

---

## 4. Updating To New Versions

The app checks for updates at startup.

When an update is available:

1. You will see an update prompt.
2. Choose **Install Now**.
3. The app downloads/installs the update and restarts.

If update does not appear:

- Ensure Google Drive is running and connected.
- Ensure you are on office network or VPN.

---

## 5. Common Issues

### App will not open

- Confirm the shared drive is mounted.
- Restart Google Drive for Desktop.
- Re-open the app.

### PIN screen appears unexpectedly

- Your activation token likely expired.
- Re-enter a current PIN from your administrator.

### Install/update blocked by Windows warning

- Use **More info** -> **Run anyway**.

---

## 6. Getting Help

If you cannot activate, update, or open the app, contact your internal support
owner and include:

- Screenshot of the error
- App version shown in the UI footer (if available)
- Approximate time of failure
